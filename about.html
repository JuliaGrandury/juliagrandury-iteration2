About Me

Originally from France and Spain, but having grown up mostly in New York, I am the product of a complex and nuanced set of experiences. As a child, I was known to never sit still. I always had to explore what was behind a door, climb to the top of a tree to get the fullest of views. I never accepted things to be one dimensioned, I had to explore every aspect of them. I connect most to all of my cultures through the experience of music and language. I don't walk from point A to point B, I dance from point A to point B. Show: trusthworthiness, dependability, resilience, and independence/initiative (want someone willing to figure shit out on their own; someone who will try to figure things out and then if they can't or if it's for something they really can't lose time on, they'll come to you) music sets the rhythm to my day i wake up to music i love tiny desk concerts and pride myself on my 65,000+ minutes listened on spotify my favorite book is The Boy, the Mole, the Fox and the Horse especially when the boy asks the horse "what is the bravest thing you've ever said?" to which the horse responds "help"

Interests

Dancing I participated in a pre-professional dance program at the School at Steps for 10 years and have continued to pursue my passion since. I have and continue to dance everything from ballet, modern, and jazz to musical theater and salsa.

Windsurfing I first started windsurfing in 2018 at the world renown French sailing school and non-profit organization Les Glénans. I have been training to become a certified windsurfing instructor ever since.

Hiking and Trekking In 2019, my sister and I set off to climb Mount Kilimanjaro - check out my travel blog here. Ever since, I have enjoyed long trekks and camping trips as well as day hikes. I have hiked parts of the Appalachian Trail and am hoping to discover the Pacific Crest Trail next.

Climbing I am top rope certified and love to climb and boulder both outdoors and indoors in my free time.

Traveling I have traveled around the world with family, friends and alone. So far, I have been to 32 countries and 5 continents. My next trips in the works are Nepal and New Zealand.

Sports I also love to engage in other sports and try My favorite of which are running, surfing, soccer, tennis, and skiing.
