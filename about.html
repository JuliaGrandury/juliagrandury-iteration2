About Me

FIX THIS PART HERE! As a child, I was known to never sit still. I always had to explore what was behind a door, test the strength of a barrier, or climb to the top of a tree to see past what everybody else saw. I never accepted things to be one dimensioned and felt the need to explore every aspect of them. This curiosity and desire to understand have remained fundamental to the person I continue to grow into and create.

Interests

Dancing I participated in a pre-professional dance program at the School at Steps for 10 years and have continued to pursue my passion since. I have and continue to dance everything from ballet, modern, and jazz to musical theater and salsa.

Windsurfing I first started windsurfing in 2018 at the world renown French sailing school and non-profit organization Les Glénans. I have been training to become a certified windsurfing instructor ever since!

Hiking & Trekking In 2017, I discovered a new passion of mine on my first camping trip. Since then I have hiked parts of the Appalachian Trail, the Camino de Compostel and in 2019, my sister and I climbed Mount Kilimanjaro for which you can check out my blog here.

Climbing I am top rope certified and love to climb and boulder both outdoors and indoors in my free time. (Yes, I usually wear shoes when I do.)

Traveling I have traveled around the world with family, friends and alone. So far, I have been to 32 countries and 5 continents. My next trips in the works are Nepal and New Zealand.

Sports I also love to engage in other sports and always push myself to try new ones. My favorites include running, surfing, soccer, tennis, and skiing.
