Interests

Hiking and Trekking In 2019, my sister and I set off to climb Mount Kilimanjaro. Ever since, I have enjoyed long trekks and camping trips as well as day hikes. I have hiked parts of the Appalachian Trail and am hoping to discover the Pacific Crest Trail next.

Climbing I am top rope certified and love to climb and boulder in my free time, both outdoors and indoors.

Traveling I have traveled around the world with family, friends and alone. So far, I have been to 32 countries and 5 continents. My next trips in the works are Nepal and New Zealand.

Sports I also engage in other sports, my favorite of which are running, surfing, soccer, tennis, and skiing.
